Mind of Divergence

Welcome to Mind of Divergence, where I, Kristopher Williams (MoD), share my journey through the realms of AI art. My approach to creativity is guided by a philosophy of seeing things differently, embracing the ever-changing landscape of self-discovery. My artwork, ranging from AI-generated pieces sold in local shops to unique postcards shared worldwide via Postcrossing, reflects my diverse interests and explorative spirit. Based in Mukilteo, WA, I invite you to explore my world of art. Discover more and connect with me through my Instagram or at kristopher.b.williams@gmail.com.
